ElaStick

A Handheld Variable Stiffness Display for Rendering Dynamic Haptic Response of Flexible Object

ElaStick is a variable-stiffness controller that simulates the dynamic response resulting from shaking or swinging flexible virtual objects

The device consists of a stiffness variable tendon that is attached to a handle. The VIVE tracker is attached to the bottom of the handle for use in VR

The variable-stiffness is mechanically controlled via a pulley system of elastick band and non-elastick strings. The motors can independantly control each of the 4 pulleys

A JND Study was conducted with a partition and noise cancelling headphones to isolate the perception of variable stiffness
