ClearVis

A Full-face Helmet Attachment for Automatic Visor Defogging

ClearVis defogs the visor of full-face helmets during low airflow situations, such as in intersections and stop and go traffic

The device attaches to the helmet via magnetic nodes which is designed to fall off during a crash to prevent neck torsion

The motors that stimulate airflow to defog the visors also charges the battery when the motorcycle is moving fast enough

An airflow sensor detects when to switch the circuit between charging mode and powering mode
