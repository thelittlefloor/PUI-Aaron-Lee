ActivEarring

Spatiotemporal Haptic Cues on the Ears

spacer
spacer
spacer
spacer

ActivEarring is capable of imparting information by stimulating three different locations on each ear

spacer

It is a haptic earring made of an electromagnet, a neodymium magnet, and a 3D-printed case. The prototype works with an external 6V power supply

spacer

A study was performed to measure the mean accuracy of each location, across locations, and the response time for different vibration frequencies

spacer

Possible applications are navigation, text to braille translations and notifications
