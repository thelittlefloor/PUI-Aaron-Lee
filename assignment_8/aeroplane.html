Aero-plane

A Handheld Force-feedback Device that Renders Weight Motion Illusion on a Virtual 2D Plane

Aero-plane is handheld device that renders weight motion illusion on a virtual 2D plane.

The device consists of 2 jet propellors that are capable of pushing out 7.1N each

The device is capable of simulating variable weight of a handheld object in VR as well as variable center of mass

There are various other ways it can be used, such as simulating the muzzle rise of a gun...

...or in AR and fishing situations
